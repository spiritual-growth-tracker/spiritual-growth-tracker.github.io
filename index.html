Install Spiritual Growth Tracker
Install this app on your device for quick and easy access:
iOS (iPhone/iPad)
  1. Open this website in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
Android
  1. Open this website in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu button in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Follow the prompts to complete installation
Windows
  1. Open this website in Microsoft Edge
  2. Click the menu button in the top right
  3. Click "Apps" and then "Install this site as an app"
  4. Click "Install" in the prompt that appears
Once installed, you can access the app directly from your home screen or app drawer, just like a native app!

Biblical Definitions: Fruits of the Spirit
Love (Agape)

Unconditional, selfless love that seeks the best for others, as demonstrated by God's love for humanity.

Joy

A deep, abiding sense of gladness and contentment that comes from knowing God, independent of circumstances.

Peace

Inner tranquility and harmony with God, others, and oneself, resulting from reconciliation with God.

Patience (Longsuffering)

Enduring difficult circumstances with a calm and positive attitude, showing forbearance toward others.

Kindness

Showing tender concern for others, being gentle and considerate in words and actions.

Goodness

Moral excellence and virtue, doing what is right and beneficial for others.

Faithfulness

Reliability, trustworthiness, and loyalty in relationships and commitments.

Gentleness (Meekness)

Strength under control, showing humility and consideration in dealing with others.

Self-control

Mastery over one's desires and impulses, exercising discipline in thoughts and actions.

Biblical Definitions: Works of the Flesh
Sexual Immorality

Any sexual activity outside of God's design for marriage between one man and one woman.

Impurity

Moral uncleanness, including impure thoughts, words, and actions.

Sensuality

Excessive indulgence in physical pleasures and gratification of the senses.

Idolatry

Worshiping or giving excessive devotion to anything other than God.

Sorcery

Practicing magic, witchcraft, or attempting to manipulate spiritual forces.

Enmity

Hostility and opposition toward others, often resulting in conflict.

Strife

Quarreling, contention, and discord in relationships.

Jealousy

Resentment of others' success or possessions, often leading to destructive behavior.

Fits of Anger

Uncontrolled outbursts of rage and violent emotions.

Rivalries

Competitive behavior that seeks to outdo others at their expense.

Dissensions

Creating division and disagreement within groups or relationships.

Divisions

Splitting groups or relationships through conflict and discord.

Envy

Desiring what others have, often leading to resentment and ill will.

Drunkenness

Excessive consumption of alcohol leading to loss of control.

Orgies

Wild, excessive parties involving immoral behavior.
